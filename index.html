The Cognitive Loop

Ah, the state of the mind!!! The brain is such a funny thing. Take for example, just a couple weeks ago, when I was trying to focus on studying but the song Beauty and A Beat just kept repeating over and over again (yes this was right after Bieberchella). Oh! And just yesterday when I was researching a former pope just to go down this whole rabbit hole about the erotic Latin novels he wrote (I was as shocked as you are!).

There's a couple different terminology for these phenomenons. I like one called cognitive loop: the rabbit hole, the earworm, the doomscroll, they're all examples of this.

It’s difficult to imagine what our minds look like while experiencing these loops. But I’d like to take you on my own take using our computer's own loopy brains. And, oh! Ever so curious Alice wants to join as well!

Before you leave, to escape each cognitive loop, you should try to find a door! Or you might be trapped in a looooooong loooooopty looooop.
